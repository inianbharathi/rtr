Details

This compact desktop AI supercomputer is powered by the NVIDIA Grace Blackwell Superchip and is purpose-built for AI prototyping, fine-tuning, and inference of large language models up to 200 billion parameters. Delivering up to 1 petaFLOP of AI performance, it provides enterprise-grade compute in a small form factor. The system includes a high-speed ConnectX-7 network interface, WiFi 7, and Bluetooth 5.3 for flexible connectivity. Preinstalled with the NVIDIA AI software stack, it enables rapid development and deployment. With dimensions of 150 × 150 × 50.5 mm and a weight of just 1.2 kg, it brings powerful AI capabilities to the desktop.
